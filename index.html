Projects

cnv2netcdf

A conversion tool developed for the Institute of Marine Research (IMR) and the EAF-Nansen Program. Reads CNV files from SBE thermosalinographs and converts them into trajectory-style NetCDF files fully compliant with the Climate and Forecast (CF) Metadata Conventions (CF-1.8), including proper standard names, units, and coordinate attributes. The repository also includes an extended quality control pipeline based on IOOS QC guidelines, with temperature, salinity, and fluorescence flagging, interactive visualisations using Plotly and Folium, and an annotated Jupyter Notebook demonstrating the full workflow.
